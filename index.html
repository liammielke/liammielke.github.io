About Kishimoto

A house built
on restraint.

Kishimoto was founded on a single conviction: that garments should outlive the moment they were made in. We design with natural materials, considered silhouettes, and an obsessive attention to construction.

We make both finished pieces and digital patterns — believing that the knowledge of how a garment is built is as valuable as the garment itself.

Our atelier is small by design. Production runs are limited. Nothing is restocked.
